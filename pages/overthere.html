OVERTHERE APP

A thoughtfully designed iOS app that helps you remember where you parked your car. Never wander parking lots again with this simple, elegant parking reminder solution.

FEATURES

  • → One-tap parking location saving
  • → Smart location detection and mapping
  • → Clean, minimal interface design
  • → Privacy-focused - all data stays on your device
  • → Works offline once location is saved

DOWNLOAD

Available now on the App Store for iPhone. Requires iOS 17.0 or later.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Built with SwiftUI and designed around the principle that the best tools are the ones you barely notice. Overthere solves a common problem with an interface so simple and intuitive that using it becomes second nature.

Every interaction has been carefully considered to minimize friction while maximizing utility. The app respects your privacy, works reliably, and gets out of your way when you need it most.
